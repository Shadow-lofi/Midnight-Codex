# Midnight Codex

**Midnight Codex** is a community of passionate, Biblically-minded programmers who love building, learning, and exploring the intersection of technology and biblical end times. Discover articles, Code Projects, and resources on how technology shapes our understanding of biblical prophecies and end times scenarios.

Join our community of tech enthusiasts and biblical scholars as we delve into the ever evolving world of technology and its implications for the end times.

## What We Do

* 💻 Build software and web applications
* 🚀 Explore new technologies and programming languages
* 🤝 Collaborate on open-source and personal projects
* 🧠 Share knowledge, ideas, and best practices
* 🌙 Code late, create more

## Our Mission

To create a welcoming space where developers & Believers alike can grow their scripture knowlege, programming skills, inspire one another, and turn creative ideas into reality through teamwork and continuous learning.

> *"Great code is written together, one commit at a time."*

Whether you're a beginner looking to learn or an experienced developer eager to contribute, **Midnight Codex** welcomes anyone with a passion for programming & biblical studying.
